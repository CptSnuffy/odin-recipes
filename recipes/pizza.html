Pizza

Description

I personally love pizza because of how simple it is to make. You can change around the type and amount of ingredients you put onto it as well.

This is a relatively simple pizza recipe. It works for anyone at any skill level. You can use homemade dough or storebought. You can also make any size portion of this recipe, just scale accordingly.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Any Extra Toppings

Steps

  1. Roll out your dough
  2. Apply your desired amount of tomato sauce
  3. Apply your desired amount of cheese
  4. Add any other toppings you might want
  5. Bake in oven at 400 degrees F for 20-22 minutes
  6. Enjoy!

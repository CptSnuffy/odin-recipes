Macaroni Salad

Description

This recipe is my grandmother's. She taught this recipe to my brother and I as a gift for graduating high school.

This pasta salad is shockingly simple for how great it tastes.

Ingredients

  • Elbow Macaroni
  • Salt & Pepper
  • One Block of Cheese
  • One Bottle of Ranch Dressing
  • One Package of Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta to your liking
  2. While the pasta is cooking cube the cheese
  3. Drain pasta and rinse with cold water
  4. Add 1/4 Cup of Ranch to pasta and mix
  5. Add cubed cheese and pepperoni
  6. Season to taste
  7. Add more ranch to your liking
